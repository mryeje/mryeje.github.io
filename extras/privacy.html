Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Local L2S Processor GPT (“the Service”) handles data provided by users.

Information We Collect

The Service only processes information that you provide directly, such as recipe JSONs, video transcripts, and related metadata. No personal information is requested or required.

How We Use Information

Submitted recipe JSONs are used to generate video clipping instructions (e.g., Shorts, TikToks, Reels). The Service may forward these JSONs to your own server if you explicitly request it (e.g., “send it for me”).

Data Retention

The Service does not permanently store your data. Processing happens in memory or temporary files only. Any saved files are under your direct control on your local server or GitHub environment.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise share your information with third parties. Any server endpoints you configure (such as via ngrok or your own hosting) are entirely under your control.

Security

While reasonable measures are taken to keep data secure during processing, please avoid providing sensitive or personally identifiable information.
