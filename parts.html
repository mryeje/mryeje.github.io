The Power of Satisfaction

The most powerful driver is the brain's response to order and problem-solving. Viewers experience a release of dopamine and serotonin when they watch chaos turn into order, a broken item fixed, or a dirty object cleaned. This creates a deeply satisfying, almost therapeutic, viewing experience that encourages re-watching and sharing.
