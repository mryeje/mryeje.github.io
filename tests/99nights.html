Mission Overview

Welcome to the QR Forest Adventure! You are an explorer in a mysterious forest filled with hidden QR codes that unlock items, challenges, and story elements.

Your mission is to explore each day, scan QR codes to collect items, and survive the forest's challenges. Each day brings new opportunities and dangers.

Use the arrows to navigate between days, and open the scanner to scan QR codes you find in the forest. Be careful - some codes might trigger enemy encounters!
